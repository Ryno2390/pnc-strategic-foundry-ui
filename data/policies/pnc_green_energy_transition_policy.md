# PNC POLICY ID: CIB-RE-2025-004
# TITLE: Green Energy Transition & Grid Storage Lending Policy

## 1. PURPOSE
To outline the underwriting standards for financing renewable energy generation (Solar, Wind) and Grid Storage projects within the Corporate & Institutional Banking (CIB) division.

## 2. CORE UNDERWRITING CRITERIA

### 2.1. Revenue Stability (The PPA Rule)
*   **Requirement:** Projects must have a secure revenue stream via a Power Purchase Agreement (PPA) or Virtual PPA (VPPA).
*   **Term:** Minimum remaining PPA term of 10 years at closing.
*   **Off-Taker Quality:** Off-taker must be Investment Grade (BBB- or better by S&P/Fitch, Baa3 by Moody's).
*   **Merchant Limit:** "Merchant Revenue" (selling energy to the spot market) cannot account for more than 20% of the total underwritten revenue stack.

### 2.2. Technology Risk & Loan-to-Value (LTV) Limits
The maximum LTV is determined by the maturity of the technology deployed.
*   **Tier 1 (Proven):** Photovoltaic Solar, On-Shore Wind, Lithium-Ion Battery Storage.
    *   *Max LTV:* 80%.
*   **Tier 2 (Emerging):** Off-Shore Wind (floating), Flow Batteries, Hydrogen Electrolyzers, Sodium-Ion.
    *   *Max LTV:* 50%.
    *   *Constraint:* Tier 2 technologies require a Manufacturerâ€™s Warranty Insurance wrapper covering performance for the loan term.

### 2.3. Interconnection Status
*   **Mandatory:** Project must have a fully executed Interconnection Service Agreement (ISA) with the RTO/ISO (e.g., PJM, ERCOT).
*   **Exclusion:** A "Queue Position" or "Feasibility Study" is INSUFFICIENT for closing. Funding cannot occur prior to ISA execution.

### 2.4. Environmental & Permitting
*   **Phase I ESA:** Clean Phase I Environmental Site Assessment required.
*   **Wetlands:** No construction permitted on designated wetlands unless mitigation credits are purchased and approved by the Army Corps of Engineers prior to closing.
