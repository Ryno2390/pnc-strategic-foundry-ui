# PNC Internal Policy: Small Business Administration (SBA) 7(a) Loan Guidelines

## Overview
The SBA 7(a) loan program is PNC's primary program for providing financial assistance to small businesses. 

## Eligibility Requirements
To qualify for an SBA 7(a) loan through PNC, the applicant must meet the following criteria:
1. **Business Type**: Must be a for-profit business operating in the United States.
2. **Size Standards**: Must meet SBA size standards (typically under $8M in annual revenue for most service industries).
3. **Credit Score**: Minimum personal credit score of 680 for all owners with 20% or more stake.
4. **Debt-to-Income (DTI)**: Maximum DTI ratio of 43% for personal guarantors.
5. **Debt Service Coverage Ratio (DSCR)**: The business must demonstrate a minimum DSCR of 1.25x based on historical or projected cash flow.

## Use of Funds
Funds may be used for:
- Expansion or renovation
- Purchase of land or buildings
- Purchase of equipment or fixtures
- Working capital
- Refinancing existing debt

## Guarantee Requirements
Personal guarantees are required from all individuals who own 20% or more of the business.
