# PNC Internal Policy: Mortgage Underwriting Guidelines - Residential

## Overview
These guidelines apply to standard residential mortgage loans for primary residences.

## Credit Requirements
- **Minimum FICO Score**: 620 for Conventional loans, 580 for FHA.
- **Bankruptcy**: Minimum 4 years since Chapter 7 discharge for Conventional; 2 years for FHA.

## Income and Employment
- **Employment History**: Minimum 2 years of continuous employment in the same industry.
- **Self-Employed**: Requires 2 years of personal and business tax returns.

## Debt-to-Income (DTI) Limits
- **Front-End Ratio**: Maximum 28% (Housing expense to gross monthly income).
- **Back-End Ratio**: Maximum 43% (Total debt obligations to gross monthly income).
- **Exceptions**: Up to 50% back-end ratio may be considered with compensating factors such as significant cash reserves or high credit score (>740).

## Loan-to-Value (LTV) Limits
- **Primary Residence**: Up to 97% LTV for qualified first-time homebuyers.
- **Standard**: 95% LTV.
- **Investment Property**: Maximum 80% LTV.
